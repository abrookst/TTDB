What is Table Turf?

Table Turf is a side mode in the game Splatoon 3, by Nintendo. In it, the chaotic turf war battles of the main Splatoon 3 game have been converted into a turn based card game. Players have 12 turns to use one of their 15 cards each turn to ink the most tiles. You can learn more about the rules of Table Turf here
